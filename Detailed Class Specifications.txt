Detailed Class Specifications
Bladedancer

Stats: Medium HP (2 hits), Fast movement
Primary Attack: Horizontal Slash

120° arc in front of player
Medium range (150 pixels)
Medium speed (300ms animation)
Hitbox active from frames 3-5


Secondary Attack: Thrust

30° narrow attack
Long range (250 pixels)
Fast speed (200ms animation)
Hitbox active from frames 2-4


Special Traits: Can move while attacking

Guardian

Stats: High HP (3 hits), Slow movement
Primary Attack: Sweeping Axe

180° arc in front of player
Medium range (130 pixels)
Slow speed (450ms animation)
Hitbox active from frames 4-8


Secondary Attack: Shield Bash

90° arc in front of player
Short range (100 pixels)
Medium speed (350ms animation)
Stuns enemies for 500ms
Hitbox active from frames 3-5


Special Traits: Reduced knockback from attacks

Hunter

Stats: Low HP (1 hit), Medium movement
Primary Attack: Bow Shot

Straight line projectile
Very long range (400 pixels)
Medium speed (350ms animation)
Arrow travels at 600px/s
Hitbox is 10x30 pixels


Secondary Attack: Dagger Slash

60° arc in front of player
Very short range (80 pixels)
Very fast speed (150ms animation)
Hitbox active from frames 1-3


Special Traits: Can see enemies from further away

Rogue

Stats: Very Low HP (1 hit), Very Fast movement
Primary Attack: Dual Dagger Strike

90° arc in front of player
Short range (110 pixels)
Very fast speed (150ms animation)
Hitbox active from frames 2-3


Secondary Attack: Dash Attack

Moves player forward 200 pixels quickly
Damages enemies in path
Medium speed (250ms animation)
Hitbox active during movement


Special Traits: Brief invulnerability during dash
